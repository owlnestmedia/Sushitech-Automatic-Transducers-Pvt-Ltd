About Us

Trusted Safety Equipment Manufacturer Since 2016

Sushitech Automatic Transducers Private Limited was established in 2016 by Er. Om Prakash Tiwari, an electronics and instrumentation engineer with a passion for industrial safety. The company was formally incorporated in 2018 and is headquartered in Vasai, Maharashtra.

We specialize in designing and manufacturing cost-effective, reliable, and innovative safety solutions for the heavy lifting industry — combining electronics, software, and mechatronics expertise to deliver products that protect lives and equipment on job sites across India.

Precision-Engineered Safety Systems

Safe Load Indicator (SLI)

Crane Load Indicator
